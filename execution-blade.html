Execution Blade

6th-level Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S, M (holy symbol)
Duration: Instantaneous
A blade of radiant light, falls from the sky in an area of 5 feets radius, dealing all creatures within that area 10d6 radiant damage, the creatures have disadvantage on any ability check they make until the end of their next turn, that blade ignores resistances and immunities. Creatures in the area can make a Dexterity saving throw, to take half damage and not suffer the effect.
