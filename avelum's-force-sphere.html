Avelum's Force Sphere

3rd-level evocation

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 120 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute
You conjure five small energy spheres in range, each about half a meter in diameter, spinning at high speed and affecting a spherical area with a diameter of 10 feet. Any creature that starts its turn or enters the area takes 3d6 force damage, or half damage with a successful Dexterity saving throw.
