Beacon of Envy

3rd-level abjuration

Casting Time: 1 reaction
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute
You become a catalyst of envy, pulling the luck of an enemy towards you. When an enemy you can see makes a roll, such as an attack or a saving throw, the target must succeed on a Charisma check. If they fail, you steal that result and store it. If you do so, the target must reroll the dice that was stolen. While maintaining concentration, you can spend the stolen value as one of your own rolls, thus ending the effect of the spell.
