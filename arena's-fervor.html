Arena's Fervor

1sd-level enchantment

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 20 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute
When you cast this spell, you instill in you and your allies the spirit of the arena. You and every willing ally within its 20ft of you has advantage on strength checks, strength saving throws and all saving throws against effects that cause the frightened condition. While a creature has this effect active, they deal +2 on all damage rolls they make, including attacks and spells, the bonus is applied to each damage caused individually before any other modifier.
