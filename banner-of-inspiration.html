Banner of Inspiration

1st-level enchantment

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous
You create a fixed, 10 feet high, spiritual banner at one location, with a symbol you are currently defending, allies within a 60-foot-radius circular area who can see the banner are inspired, and for the next 10 minutes, can add 1d6 to one ability check, attack roll, or saving throw. Each ally can only use this extra die once, ending this effect for them after it is used. For all purposes, this die counts as a bardic inspiration die. This spell can only be cast once every 8 hours.
