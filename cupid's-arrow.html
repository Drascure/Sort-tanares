Cupid's Arrow

2nd-level enchantment

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute.
You shoot a spectral arrow establishing a connection between two humanoid creatures within range (you can target yourself). Unwilling creatures must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be affected by the arrow. You may make one or both of the affected creatures to become charmed by the other targeted creature (even if that second creature succeeded on its save). This can cause two creatures to become charmed by each other, or one creature to be charmed by the other, at your choice
At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher you can charm any creature, not only humanoids.
