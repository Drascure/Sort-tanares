Aurora Heal

4th-level evocation

Casting Time: 1 bonus action
Range: Self
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute
When you cast this spell, you create an aura around you that affects a 20-foot-radius circular area. Your allies who start their turns within the area regain hit points equal to 1d6 + your proficiency bonus. Also, any conditions that are affecting creatures within the area no longer affect it at the start of your turn. Lasting effects or conditions do not cease to affect the creature, but are only suspended for the duration of the spell.
