Chorus of the Furious Animal

3rd-level Enchantment

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute
You emit a swift melody with a frenzied rhythm, provoking a beast-type creature to unleash its fury upon its foes. For the duration, the creature can make one more attack with one of his natural weapons, and become resistant to slashing, piercing, and bludgeoning damage.
