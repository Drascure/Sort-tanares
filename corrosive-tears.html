Corrosive Tears

3rd-level transmutation

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 120 feet
Components: V, S, M(a small acid flask)
Duration: Concentration, up to 30 minutes.
You conjure a green cloud that forms in the sky and small drops of corrosive acid fall from it, affecting a circular area of 30-foot radius in the range. All creatures that will start their turn or enter the affected area must make a Constitution save. On a failure the creature takes 2d8 acid damage, or half this damage on a success. Objects not being carried and structures in the affected area, take double damage.
