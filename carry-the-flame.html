Carry the Flame

5th-level Enchantment

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S, M(a sacred symbol the other person could carry)
Duration: 8 hour.
Choose a spell you are able to cast that is up to 4th level. You can grant a creature within the spell’s range the ability to cast this spell once, within this spell’s duration. The target creature uses its wisdom as the spellcasting ability for this spell and must spend the action normally used to cast it.
