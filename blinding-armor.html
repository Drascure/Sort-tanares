Blinding Armor

2nd-level evocation

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: self
Components: V, S, M(medium armor or higher)
Duration: 1 minute.
Your armor emits a strong pulse of intense light capable of blinding opponents. All hostile creatures within 15 feet of you that can see you must make a Constitution saving throw and are blinded for 1 minute in a failure. Blinded creatures can roll a save at the end of each of their turns to regain their sight.
